ABOUT US

Cityscan is part of a group specialized in the development, design, installation and operation of comprehensive technological solutions designed to solve in a reliable and efficient way the various management challenges faced by the different transport systems.

Modern societies are advancing hand in hand with the development of new infrastructure and the modernization of existing means of transport. In this way, transport must respond efficiently to meet the increasing demands of quality, reliability and safety of the service.

SIVA - audits system

The technological world has experienced a tremendous growth in recent decades, although not men´s needs have always been favored by this development. Our mission is to convert existing technological alternatives into powerful practical tools that solve specific needs of our customers in the world of transport infrastructure.

The knowledge of the possible applications of the most advanced technology, its integration into a system, its design and its implementation by means of specific creation software are the keys to our success.


Technical complexity of new technological advances requires to integrate this technology into easy use systems for the client that also solve the needs of users, operators and transport infrastructure developers in a comfortable, efficient and safe way, favoring wireless solutions and reducing hardware needs to the maximum.

Given the diversity of means of transport and the peculiarities of each of them, tools specially adapted for each case are needed, with the integration of specific hardware and software, in such a way that custom solutions are structured for each of the cases.


The development of innovative public-private collaboration schemes for the financing and construction of new infrastructure has generated the need for management control systems so that once the infrastructure is operating, compliance by private agents of agreed terms is ensured, in such a way that transport services are provided efficiently and safely.

The clients of the company are both governments and private companies that seek to implement cutting-edge technology systems that allow an easy use of the transport system for the user, as well as provide it in a safe and efficient way.
