ABOUT US

Cityscan is part of a group specialized in the development, design, installation, and operation of comprehensive technological solutions designed to reliably and efficiently solve the various management challenges faced by the different transport systems.

Modern societies are advancing hand in hand with the development of new infrastructure and the modernization of existing means of transport. In this way, transport must respond efficiently to meet the increasing demands of quality, reliability, and safety of the service.

SIVA - audits system

The technological world has experienced tremendous growth in recent decades. Our mission is to convert existing technological alternatives into powerful practical tools that solve our customers' specific needs in the world of transport infrastructure.

Knowing the possible applications of the most advanced technology, its integration into a system, its design, and its implementation utilizing specific software creation is the key to our success.


The technical complexity of new technological advances requires integrating this technology into easy use systems for the client that also solves the needs of users, operators, and transport infrastructure developers in a comfortable, efficient and safe way, favoring wireless solutions and reducing hardware needs to the maximum.

Given the diversity of means of transport and the peculiarities of each of them, tools specially adapted for each case are needed, with the integration of specific hardware and software, in such a way that custom solutions are structured for every customer.


The development of innovative public-private collaboration projects for the financing and construction of new infrastructure has generated the need for new management control systems so that once the infrastructure is operating, compliance by private agents of agreed terms is ensured in such a way that transport services are provided efficiently and safely.

The clients of the company are both governments and private companies that seek to implement cutting-edge technology systems that allow easy use of the transport system for the user and provide it safely and efficiently.
